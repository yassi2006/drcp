About the Directorate of Research Consultancy and Publication (DRCP)

The Directorate of Research, Consultancy and Publication (DRCP) is a core strategic arm of the Institute of Finance Management (IFM). Established to champion a vibrant culture of inquiry and innovation, DRCP serves as the primary engine for generating new knowledge, applying expertise to real-world challenges, and disseminating impactful findings across various sectors.

Our Mission & Vision

DRCP is guided by a clear set of principles and aspirations that drive our daily operations and long-term strategic planning.

"Mission: To foster and facilitate high-quality research, provide professional and evidence-based consultancy services, and promote the timely publication and dissemination of scholarly work, thereby contributing to national development and global knowledge."
"Vision: To be a leading center of excellence in research, consultancy, and publication, recognized for its impactful contributions to financial management, economic development, and public policy in Tanzania and beyond."

Key Functions and Objectives

To achieve our mission and vision, DRCP focuses on several key areas:

  • Promoting Research Excellence: To encourage, support, and facilitate rigorous, ethical, and relevant research among faculty, staff, and postgraduate students. This includes providing grants, training, and a conducive research environment.
  • Delivering Expert Consultancy: To offer specialized, data-driven consultancy services to government entities, the private sector, and non-governmental organizations, addressing their unique challenges and contributing to informed decision-making.
  • Enhancing Publication & Dissemination: To ensure that research findings are widely published in reputable journals, books, and other scholarly outlets, and effectively disseminated to relevant stakeholders and the public.
  • Building Research Capacity: To organize workshops, seminars, and training programs aimed at enhancing the research and consultancy skills of IFM staff and students, as well as external professionals.
  • Fostering Collaboration: To establish and maintain strong collaborations with local, regional, and international academic institutions, research organizations, and industry partners.
