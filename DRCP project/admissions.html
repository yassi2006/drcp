Admissions for Research and Postgraduate Programs

The Directorate of Research Consultancy and Publication (DRCP) collaborates closely with the IFM Admissions office to facilitate the intake of qualified candidates into our various research-oriented and postgraduate programs. We seek highly motivated individuals passionate about advancing knowledge and contributing to solutions in finance, economics, and management.

Programs Offered

DRCP supports and oversees the research components of the following programs:

  • Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) by Research: Specializations include Financial Economics, Banking & Finance, Accounting & Taxation, and Business Management.
  • Master of Science (MSc) by Research: Focused research degrees in areas such as Financial Analytics, Quantitative Finance, Public Sector Finance, and Development Economics.
  • Postgraduate Diploma in Research Methodology: A foundational program designed to equip students with advanced research skills essential for postgraduate studies and professional research.

General Eligibility Criteria

Specific requirements vary by program. Please refer to the detailed prospectus on the main IFM website. However, general criteria include:

  • PhD Programs: A relevant Master's degree from a recognized institution with a strong academic record.
  • MSc by Research Programs: A relevant Bachelor's degree with good standing (e.g., Upper Second Class or GPA of 3.0 and above).
  • Postgraduate Diploma: A relevant Bachelor's degree from a recognized institution.
  • Research Aptitude: Demonstrated interest and capability in research, often evidenced by a strong research proposal.
  • English Proficiency: Competence in written and spoken English, as all programs are conducted in English.

Application Process

  1. Review Prospectus: Download and carefully read the latest IFM Prospectus for detailed program-specific requirements and application guidelines.
  2. Online Application: All applications must be submitted through the official IFM Online Application System. (Link will be provided on IFM main site).
  3. Prepare Required Documents: Gather certified copies of academic transcripts, certificates, a detailed Curriculum Vitae (CV), two letters of recommendation, and a comprehensive research proposal (for research-based programs).
  4. Pay Application Fee: A non-refundable application fee is required. Payment details are available on the IFM Admissions portal.
  5. Interview/Selection: Shortlisted candidates for research-based programs may be invited for an interview or a research proposal defense.
