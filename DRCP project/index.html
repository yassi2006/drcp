About Directorate of Research, Consultancy and Publication

The Directorate of Research, Consultancy and Publication (DRCP) at the Institute of Finance Management (IFM) is a pivotal arm dedicated to advancing knowledge, fostering innovation, and providing solutions to contemporary challenges. Established with a mandate to promote a vibrant research culture, we strive to be a leading center for academic inquiry and practical problem-solving in finance, economics, and related fields.

Our Mission

To generate and disseminate high-quality, impactful research; to offer expert, client-focused consultancy services; and to publish scholarly works that contribute significantly to policy formulation, business practices, and societal development, both nationally and globally.

Our Vision

To be a recognized hub of excellence in research, consultancy, and publication, driving sustainable growth and innovation through informed decision-making and knowledge transfer.

Key Functions

  • Research Facilitation:Supporting faculty and students in conducting rigorous and relevant research.
  • Consultancy Services:Providing professional expertise to government, private sector, and non-governmental organizations.
  • Publication & Dissemination:Ensuring that research findings are widely published and accessible.
  • Capacity Building:Organizing workshops and seminars to enhance research and consultancy skills.
